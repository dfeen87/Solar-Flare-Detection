## **Data Sources**

### **NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center (SWPC) — GOES JSON Feeds**  
National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.  
Public API endpoints:  
- `xrays-7-day.json` — GOES X-ray flux (0.1–0.8 nm, 0.05–0.4 nm)  
- `xray-flares-7-day.json` — Flare event catalogue  
- `xray-background-7-day.json` — Quiet-Sun background  
- `magnetometers-7-day.json` — GOES magnetometer  
- `euvs-7-day.json` — EUV spectral irradiance  

### **NOAA NCEI — GOES-18 XRS Level-2 Archive**  
National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI).  
GOES‑18 XRS 1‑minute irradiance dataset (June 2022–March 2026).
